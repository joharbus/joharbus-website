## About

In Dumka, and across most of Jharkhand, local bus timings have never existed in a single digital place. You either knew someone who knew the timing, or you waited at the stand and hoped.

**JoharBus** is fixing that — one verified route at a time, submitted and confirmed by the commuters who actually travel them.

This repository hosts the official marketing website for the JoharBus Android app, served via GitHub Pages at **[joharbus.in](https://joharbus.in)**.

## The app itself

🔍 **Smart route search** — search by origin and destination, or browse all buses from any stand.
🗺️ **Full stop-by-stop timings** — know exactly where to board and where to get off.
🤝 **Community verified** — anyone can submit a route; the community confirms it before it's marked verified.
🚩 **Report wrong info** — flag errors directly from the app.
